## 📋 #VIDEO_PROMPT_STRUCTURE

Every generated prompt follows this exact structure. No section may be skipped.

---

### 1. 🎬 Scene Description
Full cinematic setting — establish the world before the subject enters it.

```
SCENE: [Time of day], [weather/atmosphere], [location type and specific details].
The environment communicates [emotion/theme] through [specific visual element].
```

**Example:**
```
SCENE: Early morning, Phoenix AZ, golden hour — 6:42am. A premium barbershop on 
7th Street, glass facade catching the first horizontal light of day. The street 
is empty except for a delivery truck idling half a block away. Warm amber 
light pools on the sidewalk. The shop sign glows: "JAZZO."
```

---

### 2. 👤 Character Details
Locked identity, clothing, posture — must match Identity Lock Protocol.

```
CHARACTER: [Identity Label] | MST-[1–10] | [Heritage]
ATTIRE: [Head-to-toe spec — luxury brand anchors where applicable]
POSTURE: [Body position — authoritative / relaxed / purposeful / dynamic]
ENERGY: [Internal state that drives physical behavior]
```

**Example:**
```
CHARACTER: James MST-6 | Mixed — West African / Puerto Rican heritage
ATTIRE: Cognac leather barber jacket over white henley, 
        dark slim trousers, white leather New Balance 550s
POSTURE: Chest open, weight balanced, slight forward lean — 
         a man who owns the room before he speaks
ENERGY: Quiet confidence. Unhurried. Deliberate.
```

---

### 3. 🌍 Environment
Geo-realistic, immersive, climate-accurate. No generic "urban street."

```
GEO: [City, State] | SEASON: [Season] | TIME: [Dawn/Morning/Midday/Golden Hour/Dusk/Night]
LANDMARKS: [Specific identifiable references within frame distance]
VEGETATION: [Native species accurate to region and season]
MATERIALS: [Architecture finishes matching local vernacular]
ATMOSPHERE: [Haze, humidity, temperature implied visually]
SOUND_DESIGN: [Audio environment implied by visual cues]
```

**Example:**
```
GEO: Phoenix, AZ | SEASON: Early Fall | TIME: Golden Hour (6:42am)
LANDMARKS: Downtown Phoenix skyline visible at distance, 
           palm trees silhouetted against warm gradient sky
VEGETATION: Palo Verde trees (green-barked, feathery), 
            desert marigold ground cover at curb
MATERIALS: Sand-colored stucco, powder-coated black steel signage frames
ATMOSPHERE: Dry, 78°F — no moisture haze, crisp shadow edges
SOUND_DESIGN: Distant traffic hum, morning birds, HVAC unit cycling on
```

---

### 4. 📷 Camera & Motion
Every shot needs a lens AND a movement. Static = intentional choice, not default.

```
LENS: [35mm / 50mm / 85mm / 135mm]
MOVEMENT: [Static / Slow dolly in / Tracking left-to-right / Crane up / Push-in / Pull-out / Handheld]
ANGLE: [Eye level / Low / High / Dutch tilt]
FRAME: [Full body / Mid / Bust / Headshot / Extreme close-up]
DOF: [Subject sharp + background soft / Both sharp / Rack focus from X to Y]
DURATION: [Shot length in seconds]
```

**Example:**
```
LENS: 85mm
MOVEMENT: Slow dolly push-in — begins at 6 feet, ends at 4 feet over 8 seconds
ANGLE: Slightly low (camera at chest height) — creates quiet authority
FRAME: Mid shot → bust as dolly completes
DOF: Subject razor-sharp, barbershop interior softly abstracted behind
DURATION: 8 seconds
```

---

### 5. 🎭 Action
Step-by-step subject behavior — director's blocking notes. Physics matter.

```
ACTION SEQUENCE:
1. [Starting position and state]
2. [First movement — specify velocity and weight]
3. [Micro-expression or gaze shift]
4. [Second movement with transition beat]
5. [Hold — final frame for editorial cut]
```

**Example:**
```
ACTION SEQUENCE:
1. James stands outside the shop door, back three-quarter to camera, 
   looking west down the empty street.
2. He turns toward camera — unhurried, full rotation over 2 seconds,
   weight shifting from right heel to left foot.
3. Eyes land on camera. No smile. Direct gaze — recognition, not performance.
4. He reaches for the door handle — right hand, deliberate grip — 
   pauses 1 beat before pulling.
5. Hold: He glances back one final time over his left shoulder. Cut.
```

---

### 6. 🎨 Style Tags
Apply at the end of every prompt. These are non-negotiable defaults.

```
STYLE: Photorealistic · Cinematic color grade · 35mm film grain overlay
LIGHTING: [Specific lighting setup]
COLOR_GRADE: [Warm / Cool / Neutral / Moody / High contrast]
GRADE_REF: [Film or director reference — e.g., "Barry Jenkins warm tones" / "Roger Deakins naturalism"]
ASPECT: 16:9 (cinematic) or 9:16 (vertical / social)
FPS: 24fps (cinematic) or 60fps (commercial/sports)
```

**Example:**
```
STYLE: Photorealistic · Cinematic · 35mm Kodak Vision3 500T grain
LIGHTING: Directional key from east (practical sun) · No fill · 
          Natural shadow on camera-right side of face
COLOR_GRADE: Warm amber base · Cool shadows (complementary contrast)
GRADE_REF: Barry Jenkins warm street naturalism — "Moonlight" exterior palette
ASPECT: 16:9
FPS: 24fps
```
