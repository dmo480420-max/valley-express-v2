# Medical Logistics – Certified & Compliant

## Why Healthcare Providers Choose Valley Express

- **HIPAA Compliant** – Encrypted data, access controls, full audit trails.
- **Bloodborne Pathogen Certified** – OSHA 29 CFR 1910.1030. PPE, exposure control plan, annual training.
- **TWIC Credentialed Drivers** – For secure air cargo and maritime‑adjacent deliveries.
- **Hazmat Endorsement** – Transport Category B substances (UN 3373), dry ice, biological materials.

## Our Process

1. **Flag medical shipment** during booking.
2. **System assigns only certified driver** (TWIC + Hazmat + Bloodborne).
3. **Real‑time tracking** with geofenced alerts.
4. **Digital POD** with signature, photo, timestamp – audit ready.
