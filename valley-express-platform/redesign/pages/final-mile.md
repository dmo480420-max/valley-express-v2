# Final Mile – White‑Glove Delivery

**Sedans** – Documents, small packages, prescription delivery.  
**Sprinter Vans** – Furniture, appliances, large retail orders.

- **Inside delivery** – We bring it to the room of choice.

- **Real‑time notifications** – SMS or email for every status change.
